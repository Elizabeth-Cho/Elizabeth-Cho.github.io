About the Project

The capstone program at Farmington High School allows students to design a project and conduct research on a topic in which they are interested. At the conclusion of the research, students are expected to create a product from the information they have collected, whether it be a research paper, a new device, or any other concept that is applicable to their project

The custom capstone is more student-led than the other capstone courses offered at the high school, as students have the ability to choose their research topic as well as decide what their product will be. As a result, they are also responsible for setting their own goals and checkpoints.

My research was focused on how digital media can be used for publicity, mainly in outreach, the spread of messages, and product sales. This information was used to create an advertisement plan for a game that I developed throughout the year.

    Mid-Year Presentation Reflection

    In this presentation, I was able to maintain a good pace throughout, although there was a moment where I had to wait a few seconds before the slide changed. In terms of speaking, I didn't speak too quickly, which allowed me to compose my thoughts as I went through the presentation. I was on the quieter side, but this could have been attributed to the fact that I was sick the day I was presenting. I did use crutch words and stutter at a couple of places throughout, which was due to the fact that I did feel a little nervous as I was presenting. One thing I noticed with my gestures was that I tend to use my left hand more than my right hand. The main thing I can do to improve for my final presentation is practice several times prior to the presentation date because I was comfortable with the content I was talking about and I did not feel like I had to spend an excessive amount of effort memorizing what I had to say. I did have notes with me, but only had to glance down at them occasionally.


    Visually, the presentation was simple and clear with little text on the slides, but because of the theme I used, some of the text was difficult to read from the audience. In my final presentation, I will need to make sure there is enough contrast between the text and the background so it is readable from a distance.
