Digital Media and Publicity

Driving question

How is digital media utilized in order to increase publicity, such as in outreach, the spread of a message, or product sales?

Focus of project

Computer science

Description:

The program I am currently in is custom capstone, which consists of an independently led project. Students are required to develop a driving question, conduct research, and create a final product. While doing so, they must set their own pace in order to complete the project on time.

My project looks at how digital media, such as websites, social media, and graphic design, are used to draw people in and generally get the word out to the public. This includes looking at which social media platforms are better for which demographics and what kind of language draws people in. This information will be utilized to advertise an online game I create with the aid of others that contains a social or political message.
