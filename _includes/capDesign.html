Driving Question

How is digital media utilized in order to increase publicity, such as in community outreach, the spreading of a message, or product sales?

Goals/Learning Targets

Personal: I want to improve my communication skills, which involves being able to communicate my ideas, questions, and concerns with professionals from the field and speaking in front of a group.

Content: I want to learn about what makes certain marketing and publicity strategies more appealing/effective than others.

Skills: I want to improve my software design workflow using GitHub, a site where people can post code and keep track of projects, which includes using branches, opening issues, and referring to them in commits that are dedicated to resolving said issue.

Anticipated Outcomes/Products

My product will be a choose-your-own-adventure style online game with a political or social commentary, which will be advertised through social media using the information gathered from research and interviews.

Potential Audiences

  • Anyone who enjoys choose-your-own-adventure style games or stories with a social or political commentary
  • People within the age range of 14 to 18
