Name: Chelsea Shasta

Occupation: Student

Description: 5'5", brown hair, blue eyes, 18 years old

Biography

Born in Saphira, Chelsea comes from a family of four, with her twin brother, Chris, her mother, and father. Her two closest friends are Ruby and Alex. The three often spend time at the local cafe or just walking around town. She is currrently a candidate for the campaign for the Elemental position.

Name: Flurry Hex

Occupation: Candidate

Description: 5'3", blonde hair, blue eyes, 17 years old

Biography

Flurry and her twin, Crystal, come from Citrine. In their hometown, they are known as dance prodigies. Their family is known for their discipline. Outside of dance, Flurry is a classical pianist. She, along witih Crystal, are candidates in the campaign for the Elemental position.

Name: Lucas Blackmore

Occupation: Candidate

Description: 5'7", black hair, brown eyes, 18 years old

Lucas is from Achroite and is particularly skilled with technology. Name: Kevin Campbell

Occupation: Candidate

Description: 5'5", black hair, brown eyes, 18 years old

Biography

Kevin comes from a family of three in Ammonite. In his hometown, he is primarily known for his athleticism in tennis, but has also made his name known in academics. Although he is not particularly interested in the world of politics, his friends and community convinced him to get involved. He is currently a candidate in the campaign for the Elemental position.

Name: Jade Li

Occupation: Elemental Tactical

Description: 5'5", black hair with red tips, brown eyes, 21 years old

Biography

Jade comes from Ammonite but currently resides in the capital of Amethyst as an Elemental Tactical. As the commanding officer of the Tacticals, her loyalties lie strictly with the Elemental Kingdom. She spends much of her time with Jasper and Onyx, leaders in their respective areas.

Name: Onyx Arcane

Occupation: Elemental Specialist

Description: 5'6", black hair, brown eyes, 21 years old

Biography

Onyx comes from Ammonite but currently resides in the capital of Amethyst as an Elemental Specialist. As a leader of the Specialists, he mainly deals with training the new recruits. He spends much of his time outside of training with Jade and Jasper, leaders in their respective areas.

Jasper comes from Ammonite but curently resides in the capital of Amethyst as an Elemental Storm. As the commanding officer of the Storms, she mainly oversees operations. She spends much of her time with Onyx and Jade, leaders in their respective areas.
